Research Assistant - Bayesian Belief Distillation for Negotiation Agents

First-author research | Bytes of Minds Lab, Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai | Advisor: Prof. Baihan Lin | NeurIPS 2026 submission, under review

  • Designed a language-as-likelihood, Bayes-as-update framework in which a Llama-3.3-70B teacher scores dialogue compatibility against six opponent priority orderings to produce calibrated, turn-level posteriors over hidden preferences in CaSiNo campsite negotiation.
  • Distilled the Bayesian teacher into a Llama-3.1-8B student via LoRA SFT; the student emits auditable tagged outputs for posterior, intent, content, and utterance while preserving belief calibration on 1,054 held-out turns.
  • Established a same-scale supervised baseline that exceeds prior CaSiNo SOTA under matched 5-fold cross validation.
  • Diagnosed pre-registered null results, including SVO swap-test insensitivity within fixed lambda regimes and Big Five style-gate failure, separating lambda-scale effects from social-label assignment effects.
  • Built belief-policy decomposition, posterior-trajectory visualizations, and posterior-prefix interventions to make weak belief-action coupling visible.
  • Stack: Llama-3.1 / 3.3, LoRA and QLoRA via TRL, PyTorch, HPC with LSF on Arion / Minerva, A100 / H100.

Drug Efficacy Modeling and LLM-Based Explainability

Industry-supervised research | SK Life Science

  • Reproduced and extended a Data-driven Explanatory Model using Random Forest on 311K drug-cell-line pairs across 2,024 multi-omic features from CTRP, CCLE, and PubChem; achieved R2 about 0.66 and Pearson r about 0.82 on held-out CTRP data.
  • Engineered a custom perturbation kernel based on Random Forest leaf-agreement with weighted tree logic and depth-aware scaling to model drug-tumor similarity for downstream treatment-response inference.
  • Built an end-to-end SHAP-to-LLM explainer that extracts TreeSHAP attributions, maps features to biomedical metadata, and assembles structured prompts for grounded natural-language explanations of IC50 predictions.
  • Benchmarked Llama 3.1 8B, Qwen 2.5 32B, BioMistral 7B, and Meditron 7B on explanation quality; designed a QLoRA fine-tuning pipeline targeting H100 GPUs with GGUF export for local inference.
  • Co-designed a blinded expert-evaluation framework using stratified drug-cell pairs and forced-choice paired comparison across full-feature, SHAP-only, shuffled-SHAP, and wrong-drug explanation variants.
